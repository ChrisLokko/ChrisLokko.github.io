About Us

PREMIER LOGISTICS is a privately held Puerto Rican Corporation that continues to grow rapidly in the market place. Juan Carlos Franco, founder of PREMIER LOGISTICS, started as an air freight broker and has grown PREMIER into one of Puerto Rico’s leading LOGISTICS Companies; offering customized and integrated end-to-end logistics services. Major investments in computer technology and infrastructure enhancements enable PREMIER LOGISTICS to consistently develop service capabilities on several fronts. PREMIER LOGISTICS is a third party logistics and transportation management company, committed to bringing our partners products safely along the way. We specialize in understanding our customer’s needs and concerns, working each day to meet the highest standards of service. That is why here at PREMIER LOGISTICS we value Reliability, Loyalty, Integrity and Honesty. Here in PREMIER LOGISTICS we offer more than 40+ Rigs (Kenworth, Freightliner, Ford, Volvo, Mack and GMC), Delivery Fleet equipped with lift gates, 97% - 99% Delivery Accuracy, Constant Monitoring and Communication with Drivers, Fully Uniformed Drivers. We are prepared to manage Furniture, Air conditioning products, Hazmat products, General store products, Liquids, Beauty supplies, Appliances, Hardware Construction products.
